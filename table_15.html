The decomposition of nitrates

Nitrates are decomposed when heated. The composition of the decomposition products depends on the activity of the metal, the cation of which is part of the nitrate, i.e., from its position in the series of standard electrode potentials (the electrochemical series):

K   Na   Mg   Al   Zn   Fe   Ni   Sn   Pb   (Н2)   Cu   Hg   Ag   Pt   Au
The position of the metal in the electrochemical series The reaction products Examples

To the left of Mg

Metal nitrite and oxygen

 2NaNO3 =t° 2NaNO2 + O2↑

Between Mg and Сu

Metal oxide, nitrogen dioxide and oxygen

 2Сu(NO3)2 =t° 2CuO + 4NO2↑ + O2↑

To the right of Сu

Metal, nitrogen dioxide and oxygen

 AgNO3 =t° Ag + 2NO2↑ + O2↑

Ammonium ion

Nitrous oxide and water

 NH4NO3 =t° N2O + 2H2O
